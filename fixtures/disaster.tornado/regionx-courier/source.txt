Tornado Damage Assessment Under Way in Coastal City

Date: February 19, 2024

Location: Coastal City, Region X, Country Y

A tornado struck Coastal City on the evening of February 18, the regional emergency office confirmed. Two people were killed and at least 14 were injured, the county sheriff said on Monday morning. The emergency office said 37 homes were destroyed and search operations concluded on Sunday evening. The National Weather Bureau assigned the tornado a preliminary EF3 rating.
