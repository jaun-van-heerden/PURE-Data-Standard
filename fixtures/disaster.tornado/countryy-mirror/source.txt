Coastal City Tornado Kills Two, Wrecks Dozens of Homes

Date: February 19, 2024

Location: Coastal City, Region X, Country Y

Two people were killed and at least 14 were injured when a tornado tore through Coastal City on February 18, according to a report by the Region X Courier. The Courier report, citing the county emergency office, said 37 homes were destroyed. The National Weather Bureau assigned the tornado a preliminary EF3 rating, the report said.
